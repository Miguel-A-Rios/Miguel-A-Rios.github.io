Artifitial Intelligence is a branch within computer science concerned with creating machines that are able to learn, make decisions, and do endless different types of tasks at a human level. AI does this by taking in a myriad of data, processsing it, and learning from past behavior to improve. There advanced forms of AI machines that can learn and grow on their own, independently without human intervention. Even the more basic forms of AI can handle complicated tasks that usually need human touch to fix small erros, so it could need help from a programmer to learn from its mistakes and imporve.
