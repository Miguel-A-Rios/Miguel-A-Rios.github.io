The beginnings of AI dates as far as the 1950s and 1960s, where the the phrase Artifitial Intelligence and the idea of it was created. From programming languages that are still used today, to movies exploring the idea of robots. During the AI boom from 1980 to 1987, there was rapid growth and interest in AI that came in forms of research breaktroughs with government funding to support researchers. There were deep learning techniques and use of Expert Systems gained popularity which allowed computers to learn from mistakes and make indepedent decisions.
Fast forward to 2012-present day, theres been a big increase in common use AI tools, such as virtual assistance, search engines, deep learning and big data which is used in OpenAI, and GPT-4, etc. AI is part of evryday life for both businesses and consumers. It is commonly used either through social media, online shopping, search engines, and much more.
